CRITIC APPLICATION

Please follow the instructions below to apply to become an approved Cinema Paradiso Critic.

  • Register for a Cinema Paradiso account if you haven't already. Click here to register. This is a required step.
  • Send an email to CinemaParadiso308@gmail.com with the subject Film Critic Submission Application and the following information:
    • Your Name
    • Your Email Address
    • Source Name (publications, web site, TV/radio station)
    • Source URL
    • URL to an index of your film reviews
    • URLs to at least three film reviews you've authored
    • Critics Society or Association
    • Email address you registered with

Questions? Contact us here.



SOURCE ADMINISTRATION APPLICATION

Editors and webmasters of publications which are already contributing the Rating System are encouraged to apply for "source administration" privileges. Once authorized, editors may log into Rotten Tomatoes in order to add, edit, or delete articles and reviews from their publication on Cinema Paradiso. This allows editors to maintain their listings on Cinema Paradiso for multiple authors.


Please follow the below instruction to apply:

  • Register for a Cinema Paradiso account if you haven't already. Click here to register. This is a required step.
  • Send an email to CinemaParadiso308@gmail.com with the subject Source Administration Application and the following information:
    • Your Name
    • Your Email Address
    • Your Job Title
    • Source Name (publications, web site, TV/radio station)
    • Source URL
    • Email address you registered with

Questions? Contact us here.
